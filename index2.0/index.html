about me

My name is Nichole Pellow, and I am a front-end developer. I live in a tiny town in East Tennessee that is nestled on the southwest banks of the Little Tennessee River while being surrounded by both the Unicoi and Great Smoky Mountains. I am originally from southeast Georgia and have lived in various other places stretching from the southeast to the midwest and even the UK. I studied at Purdue University and obtained a B.S. in Computer Science with a focus in web development. Currently, I am working towards increasing my skill set by learning C# and React. My front-end development interests include responsive design, UI design, and continuous learning to keep up with design and development trends.
