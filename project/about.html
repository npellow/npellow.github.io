About Us

Moon Child Decor started in 2001 as a hobby among a group of friends that loved bohemian designs. The hobby quickly grew into a small business when they started sharing their boho chic designs online and others became interested in how they could get the stylish look in their own homes.

By 2005, their local shop opened its doors, and by 2010 they started selling online. The first Moon Child Decor online shop started as a small Etsy store, and within two years needed to be expanded into a online retail store.

The creators of Moon Child Decor adore the boho chic styles, their vendors, and customers!
